Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how our Duty Hour Tracking App ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you or your employer uses our services. By using the App, you agree to the terms described below.

Information We Collect

  • Profile Information: Name, email, phone number, and other basic details provided during account setup or profile updates.
  • Attendance & Location Information: We collect WiFi SSID, IP address, and device connection details when you connect to your workplace network. This data is used strictly to verify attendance (check-in/check-out).
  • Usage Information: Data about how you use the App, including shift details, preferences, and subscription usage.
  • Subscription & Billing Information: Business owners’ payment details and subscription history (processed securely via third-party providers like Stripe).

How We Use Your Information

  • Verify employee attendance using workplace WiFi connection.
  • Generate accurate duty hour reports for employees and employers.
  • Provide subscription-based access to business owners and manage billing securely.
  • Improve app functionality, performance, and security.
  • Communicate with users about their account, subscriptions, and service updates.

Location & WiFi Data

Your WiFi SSID, IP address, and connection details are collected only to confirm whether an employee is present at a registered workplace location. We do not share this data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Users can disable location or WiFi permissions from their device settings, but doing so may limit the core functionality of the App.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We continuously update our systems to minimize risks.

Data Retention

We retain personal and attendance data as long as necessary to provide services to your employer and to comply with legal obligations. Employers may also configure retention periods based on organizational policies.

Children's Privacy

Our App is intended for workplace use only and is not designed for individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Subscription & Payments

Business owners subscribe to access the platform. Payment details are processed securely by trusted third-party providers, and we do not store sensitive card information on our servers.
